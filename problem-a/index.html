Kocho Kanae

Kocho Kanae is a late Flower Hashira from the Demon Slayer Manga and Anime series. She is the eldest sister of Shinobu Kocho and Kanao Tsuyuri. Kanae is known to be extremely easygoing and believed that demons could be saved.
